Government

Members of the Ul Dynasty claim their sovereignty by right of birth. Their will is carried out by the Divan, a group of eight viziers who oversee matters of law, finance, engineering, the interior, commerce, war, religious affairs, and palace affairs. However the influence of the sultans has been waning for some time, and at the present true power is wielded by the Syndicate, a council sat by six of Ul’dah’s most elite and influential.

Gallery

The Chamber of Rule in Ul'dah is the opulent seat of the Sultana, where power and wealth intertwine. Adorned with lavish decor, it serves as a stage for political intrigue, with influential figures vying for favor amidst whispers of ambition and betrayal. Here, the Sultana balances governance and the needs of her citizens while navigating the ever-present schemes that threaten the city's stability.

The Aetheryte Crystal in Ul'dah serves as a vital hub for adventurers and citizens alike, facilitating instantaneous travel throughout Eorzea. This towering crystal, glowing with ethereal light, is not only a symbol of the city's prosperity but also a testament to the technological marvels of the Allagan Empire. Adventurers gather here to connect with distant locations, while the surrounding area bustles with merchants and travelers, all drawn to the crystal’s enchanting aura and the promise of new journeys.

The Sapphire Avenue Exchange is the bustling heart of commerce in Ul'dah, renowned for its vibrant markets and wealth of trade opportunities. This grand structure, adorned with gleaming gemstones, attracts merchants and adventurers alike, eager to buy and sell goods from across Eorzea. Here, the clamor of haggling fills the air as fortune seekers navigate the lively stalls, making it a lively focal point of Ul'dah's economic prosperity.

The Quicksand is a renowned tavern in Ul'dah, serving as a popular gathering place for adventurers and locals alike. With its warm, welcoming atmosphere, the tavern is a hub for sharing stories, forming alliances, and seeking work. Patrons enjoy hearty meals and refreshing drinks while exchanging rumors of quests and treasures, making it an essential stop for those looking to immerse themselves in the city's vibrant culture and camaraderie.

The Goblet is a vibrant residential district in Ul'dah, serving as a key housing area for adventurers and citizens alike. Known for its beautifully designed homes and lush surroundings, this locale fosters a sense of community among its residents. The Goblet offers various amenities, including shops and social spaces, making it a popular choice for those looking to settle down amidst the bustling life of the city while enjoying the comforts of home.

The Gold Saucer is a renowned amusement park and entertainment hub in Eorzea, famous for its dazzling attractions and thrilling games. Nestled in the Twilight Forest, this sprawling complex features a variety of activities, from chocobo racing to innovative mini-games, drawing adventurers from all over to test their luck and skills. With its vibrant atmosphere, lively music, and enticing prizes, the Gold Saucer is the ultimate destination for fun and leisure, offering endless excitement and opportunities to unwind amidst the adventures of the realm.
